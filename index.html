Boom Buddy Challenge

Boom Buddy Training  ·  Group Activity  ·  10 Minutes

"What you know as wellbeing must touch every human being." — Sadhguru
Run of Show — 10 Minutes Total
TimeWhat HappensDurationAction
0:00 – 1:00 Facilitator gives one example idea to spark thinking. Hand out slips."4 minutes. One idea. Make it real — something you'd actually do this week." 1 min Start
1:00 – 5:00 Groups brainstorm. One person writes, everyone fires ideas. Focus: easy, creative, high reach. 4 mins Brainstorm
5:00 Bell or clap signal. Groups lock in their ONE best idea and pick a spokesperson. — Lock In
5:00 – 8:00 Each spokesperson stands and pitches in 45 seconds. Idea + reach estimate. 4 groups × 45 sec. 3 mins Present
8:00 – 9:00 Clap-o-meter vote. Facilitator calls each group — crowd claps — ~10 sec each."Streak Freaks… Insight Seekers… Mood Masters… Mind Explorers…" ~1 min Vote
9:00 – 10:00 Close with energy."Every one of these ideas could reach thousands. Save it in your phone right now — and do it this week." 1 min Close
Before This Segment — Things to Prep
1Groups pre-assigned before people sit — don't let them self-select
2Hand out slips right as this segment begins
3Someone watching the clock — signal the group at 5:00
Groups: Streak Freaks · Insight Seekers · Mood Masters · Mind Explorers

Streak Freaks
Boom Buddy
Challenge
Your 4-Minute Challenge
Example to spark ideas
A restaurant puts a small sign or flyer on the table — "Don't wait. Meditate." — reaching diners during the 10-15 minutes they wait for their food. Simple, near-zero cost, high reach.
1
Think — who could benefit from 7 minutes of meditation?
2
Where or when would they be most open to hearing about it?
3
What's one creative, simple way to reach them?
4
How many people could this reach in one week?
⏱ 4 min brainstorm  ·  45 sec to present 1 person presents  ·  clap-o-meter votes
